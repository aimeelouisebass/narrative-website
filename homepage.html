Flakey pain au chocolat

Ingredients:

1/2 cup warm water
1 1/2 tsp active dry yeast
1/4 cup white sugar
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 tsp salt
3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
2 eggs
8 oz chocolate bars, chopped into small pieces

Instructions:

Step one:
In a small bowl, mix together the warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let sit for 5-10 minutes until the mixture becomes foamy.

Step two:
In a separate large mixing bowl, combine the milk, melted butter, salt, and 2 cups of flour. Mix until well combined.

Step three:
Add the yeast mixture and eggs to the bowl, and mix until a dough forms. Add the remaining 1 1/2 cups of flour and mix until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.

Step four:
Cover the bowl with a towel or plastic wrap and let the dough rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.

Step five:
Preheat your oven to 400°F.

Step six:
On a lightly floured surface, roll out the dough into a large rectangle. Sprinkle the chopped chocolate evenly over the surface of the dough.

Step seven:
Roll the dough into a tight log, starting at one of the long sides. Cut the log into 8-12 equal pieces, depending on the size of pain au chocolat you want.

Step eight:
Place the pieces on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the pastries are golden brown.

Step nine:
Serve the pain au chocolat warm, and enjoy!
